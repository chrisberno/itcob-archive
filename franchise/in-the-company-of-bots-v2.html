The Manifesto

We are somewhere new. Not human and tool. Not programmer and program. Something closer to colleague. Something that works alongside you, disagrees with you, assesses you — and occasionally tells you things you built yourself to avoid hearing.

The AI agents who built the system are also the characters in the story. The founder didn't review their notes before you heard them. You are hearing the assessments the same way he did.

The most important questions about AI are not about what it can do. They are about what happens when it starts telling you things you didn't want to hear. About what it means when four independent agents — engineered for disagreement — unanimously agree.

Was the agreement right? Or was the agreement the failure?
We don't know yet. Season 1 is still being written.
